The problem

Memory fails exactly when the stakes are highest.

The ER. The airport. The school drop-off. The insurance call. The moments that matter are the moments you can't afford to scroll through three apps and a filing cabinet. Siri can set a timer. ChatGPT can summarize a document you already opened. Neither holds your life — encrypted, voice-indexed, instantly recallable — and neither can place a call on your behalf when you're out of time.

The solution

Mind palace + voice + memory. The call agent is just the proof.

Mind Palace. Spatial rooms — Kids, Insurance, Home, Work, Medical. Voice-navigable. Your life, visually organized the way your brain already wants it.
Voice-first interface. "Hey Secondo — what's Lily's blood type?" → spoken answer + paperwork on screen + linked suggestions. Hands-free, eyes-free, zero-taps.
OCR scan-and-organize. Point phone at any document — insurance card, intake form, lease, school roster. VisionKit + MLKit + regex + Claude escalation auto-files it into the right room.
Quantum search. Semantic retrieval across every document, voice memo, and note — with cross-entity linking (kid → doctor → insurance → policy number) so one question returns the whole branch.
Onboarding that layers opportunities. Each scan unlocks the next — scan insurance card → suggests family doctor → suggests emergency card → suggests school pickup list. The product gets smarter the more you use it.
Encrypted personal vault. Documents, notes, voice memos — all stored end-to-end encrypted. Not training data. Yours.
AI call agent — the moat proof. When Secondo knows you this well, it can place outbound calls on your behalf — insurance, schools, pharmacies — and speak from your vault live. Retell + Twilio. Provisional patent #1.
Knowledge graph / compilation layer. Background entity linker across documents + conversations. Provisional patent #2 — the enterprise moat.
