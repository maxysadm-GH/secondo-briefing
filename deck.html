Why this exists

The insurance call that takes 47 minutes. Every time.

Every professional has the same drawer: policy numbers, lease dates, kid's pediatrician intake forms, wi-fi passwords, tax PDFs. The information exists. But when you're on hold with Geico and they ask for your policy number, you're flipping through three apps and a filing cabinet.

"I don't need another note-taking app. I need the note to answer the phone for me."

Secondo is the system that scans your documents, holds them encrypted, and — when the moment comes — picks up the call and speaks on your behalf, with your vault open.

The solution

A voice-first second brain that shows up when you can't.

AI call agent. Initiates outbound calls (Retell AI + Twilio); answers on your behalf with live vault access. Provisional patent.
Encrypted personal vault. Documents, notes, voice memos — stored encrypted, queried by the agent mid-call.
Scan-and-organize. VisionKit + MLKit OCR pipeline; regex + Claude tier-escalation for structured fields.
Mind Palace. Spatial rooms (Lease, Insurance, Kids, Work) — voice-navigable, FAB-organized.
Voice-first interface. "When does my lease expire?" → spoken answer. "Call Geico about the claim" → placed call.
Persona registry. Specialized agent library — meeting liaison, debate orchestrator, morning briefing.
Context HUD. Real-time floating overlay surfaces vault snippets during active calls.
Knowledge graph. Background entity linker across documents + conversations. Patent #2 foundation.
